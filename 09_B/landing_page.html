Aliens in Action: Online Typing Experiment

Why ?

The purpose of this study is to assess typing abilities in younger children. You are part of the control group, so don't be puzzled by the simplicity of the task.

How ?

This experiment will measure your typing speed in different contexts. The data collected is completely anonymous. You can decide to leave the experiment at any point. The data is only collected at the very end of the experiment. For the reliability of our data, we would ask you to only participate once.

Who are we ?

This study was developed and carried out by members of a research unit at Heinrich-Heine-University.
